## ğŸš€ Getting Started

### What is Pathfinder?

Pathfinder is an AI-powered platform that transforms multi-family group trip planning into a streamlined, collaborative experience. Whether you're planning a beach vacation with extended family or a ski trip with friends, Pathfinder helps you:

- **Gather everyone's preferences** using intelligent polls
- **Generate AI-powered itineraries** that balance everyone's needs
- **Coordinate in real-time** with built-in chat and activity feeds
- **Track shared expenses** with automatic settlement suggestions

### System Requirements

- **Modern web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge - latest versions)
- **Microsoft account** for secure authentication
- **Internet connection** (mobile data or WiFi)

### Mobile Experience

Pathfinder is a Progressive Web App (PWA), meaning:
- **Install on your home screen** for app-like experience
- **Works on any device** - phones, tablets, computers
- **Offline access** to view trip details (coming soon)

## ğŸ¤– AI-Powered Itinerary Generation

### How AI Generation Works

Pathfinder's AI analyzes:
- **Poll results** and voting patterns
- **Individual preferences** from all travelers
- **Budget constraints** across families
- **Destination information** from trusted sources

Then generates a balanced itinerary that maximizes group satisfaction.

### Generating an Itinerary

1. Go to your trip's **"Itinerary"** tab
2. Ensure key polls are complete (destination, dates, activities)
3. Click **"Generate AI Itinerary"**
4. Wait 30-60 seconds for the AI to create your plan

### Reviewing & Editing

The AI generates:
- **Day-by-day schedule** with activities
- **Accommodation suggestions** matching your budget
- **Transportation recommendations**
- **Alternative options** for flexibility

You can:
- **Drag and drop** activities to rearrange
- **Delete** activities you don't want
- **Add custom activities** manually
- **Request regeneration** with different parameters

### Consensus Score

Each itinerary element shows a **Consensus Score** (0-100%):
- ğŸŸ¢ **80-100%**: Strong agreement
- ğŸŸ¡ **50-79%**: Moderate agreement
- ğŸ”´ **Below 50%**: Potential conflict - consider discussion

## ğŸ’¡ Tips & Best Practices

### For Trip Organizers

1. **Start early** - Give families time to respond to polls
2. **Set clear deadlines** - Poll deadlines encourage participation
3. **Use AI suggestions** - Let the AI generate starting points for discussion
4. **Keep everyone informed** - Use the chat for quick updates

### For Families

1. **Complete preferences early** - Helps AI generate better suggestions
2. **Vote in all polls** - Your input shapes the itinerary
3. **Check in regularly** - Real-time updates keep you in the loop
4. **Log expenses promptly** - Easier to track than reconciling later

### For Large Groups

1. **Assign family admins** - Distributed responsibility works better
2. **Use category polls** - Break big decisions into smaller votes
3. **Set ground rules early** - Budget limits, activity levels, etc.
4. **Plan buffer time** - Large groups need flexibility

## â“ Troubleshooting

### I can't sign in

- **Clear browser cache** and try again
- **Use a different browser** temporarily
- **Check your Microsoft account** is active
- **Contact support** if issues persist

### The AI isn't generating itineraries

- **Complete required polls** first (destination, dates minimum)
- **Ensure enough responses** - AI needs data to work with
- **Try regenerating** with different parameters
- **Check internet connection**

### I'm not seeing real-time updates

- **Refresh the page** to reconnect WebSocket
- **Check internet stability** - unstable connections cause delays
- **Try a different browser** if issues continue

### Expenses don't add up

- **Check currency settings** - mixing currencies causes confusion
- **Verify who paid vs. who benefited**
- **Review split calculations** manually

### Someone can't join my trip

- **Verify their email** is correct in the invitation
- **Ask them to check spam** folder
- **Resend the invitation** if expired
- **Add them to a family first** if they're not in one
